The Mission

How It Works

Four steps from empty room to legendary hacker.

01

Claim Your Room

Sign up with Hack Club. You instantly get a room inside the Safehouse — four walls, empty. That's your base. Decorate it, fill it, make it yours.

02

Build Something Real

A game, a website, a tool, an art project — anything you're genuinely excited about. Log your hours as you go. The more you build, the more you earn. No faking it.

03

Ship It to the Wall

Submit your project. It gets reviewed, and if it ships — your project card gets pinned to your personal Wall of Fame inside the Safehouse HQ. Your room literally grows around your work.

04

Collect BBQ & Upgrade

Every shipped project earns BBQ Coins. Spend them in the BBQ Shop on real swag, HQ decorations, and exclusive Safehouse items you literally cannot get anywhere else.

The Currency

What Is a BBQ Coin?

BBQ Coins are the Safehouse's official currency. No grinding, no loopholes — you earn them by actually building things.

Rule 1

1 hour of real work = 1 BBQ Coin

Log the time you spend coding, designing, writing, or building. Be honest. That's all that's required.

⏱
Rule 2

Ship a project = bonus coins on top

Every completed and publicly shipped project earns you a bonus stack of coins on top of your hourly tally.

Rule 3

Spend coins in the BBQ Shop

Swap your coins for real-world swag, HQ room upgrades, secret items, and Hall of Fame status.
